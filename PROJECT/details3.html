Activities

Joining a club or group can provide opportunities for social interaction and shared interests. Here are some places you can go:
  • Thor: This is a gym for that has groups for old people.
  • Aleco: This place has a gym but also outdoor activities if the gym doesnt suit you.
  • dstraining: This place also has a gym and outdoor activities and people who can give 1 on 1 training.

Volunteer

Volunteering is a great way to stay active and engaged in your community. Here are some organizations that welcome older volunteers:
  • Roosendaal Voor Elkaar: This website has wide range of groups you can join. Whether to help kids or helping the disabled.
  • Elisabeth Roosendaal: Here you can volunteer to help people within healthcare.
  • We Are Roosendaal: This is to help the neighborhood. Whether it is to provide help for with computer, doing odd jobs, or do errands.
