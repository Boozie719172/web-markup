Physical activities

  • Walking: This is a simple and effective exercise that can be done anywhere.
  • Yoga: Yoga is a good calm exercise that improves flexibility, balance, and strength.
  • Gardening: Gardening is a activity that provides a sense of accomplishment. Not just helping you physically but also mentally. It's also a great way to enjoy the outdoors.
  • Dancing: Dancing is a fun way to stay fit and active. It helps improve your balance.
